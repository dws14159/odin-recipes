Lamb Tagine

Description

An excellent stew that has a delightfully sweet start and a piquant finish.

Ingredients

  • Lamb
  • A whole pile of other stuff

Steps

  1. Lamb and olive oil into a bowl.
  2. Herbs into a bag, add the lamb, coat well and bung in the fridge for 8 hours.
  3. Cook the lamb in a large heavy-bottomed pot for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add onions and carrots etc and stir.
  5. Cover and simmer for a couple of hours until the meat is tender.
  6. Serve over couscous.
