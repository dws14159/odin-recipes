Scrambled Egg

Description

Use your microwave to make light and fluffy scrambled eggs for a quick and easy breakfast to start your day.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Break the eggs into a microwave-proof mixing bowl. Add milk and salt and stir well.
  2. Nuke for 30 seconds and whisk.
  3. Repeat until the eggs have the desired consistency.
